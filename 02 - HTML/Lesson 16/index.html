Latest Instagram updates

  • Grid Reordering: You can now rearrange posts on your profile grid by long-pressing
    a post and selecting Reorder Grid to freely drag and drop content without deleting
    and reposting.

  • Photos in Comments: You can now share images directly from your gallery in Reel
    comment sections, alongside standard text and GIFs.

  • Carousel Edits: Post-publish updates let you reorder slides in your carousels and
    toggle between single or multiple captions.

  • Original Content Protection: Accounts that primarily repost content from creators
    will lose recommendation status on the Explore and discovery feeds.

  • Algorithm Customization: "Your Algorithm" allows you to prioritize or remove specific
    topics directly from your main feed and Explore pages.

  • AI Creator Labels: Meta has begun rolling out account-level "AI Creator" tags for accounts
    that frequently produce or modify content using AI.

Safety tips

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add a critical layer of defense by requiring an SMS or authenticator
    app (like Google Authenticator) code when logging in from an unrecognized device.

  • Security Alerts: Toggle on Login alerts via Settings > Messages and story replies > Security alert so you
    are immediately notified if an unauthorized person accesses your profile.

  • Avoid Risky Apps: Never use third-party "follower growth" or "auto-like" bots. Granting them access
    compromises your login credentials.

  • Avoid Spam Flags: Do not follow, unfollow, or send DMs to a high volume of accounts in rapid succession,
    as this can trigger temporary account restrictions.
